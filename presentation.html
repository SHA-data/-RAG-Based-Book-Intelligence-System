Backend Architecture Insight

📚 Book Ingestion Pipeline

The system robustly maps uploads sequentially. Incoming PDF, TXT, and EPUB documents pass through the multi-format Parser, executing text isolation with page and chapter metadata preservation. Content is heavily cleansed and smartly overlapping via chunker.py.

EbookLib PyMuPDF BeautifulSoup4

🧠 Neural Vector Store

Processed chunks are computationally embedded utilizing models/text-embedding-004 through the Gemini SDK. High-dimensional vector mappings are stored locally via a persistent ChromaDB instance allowing hyper-fast semantic querying mapped accurately by chunk metadata coordinates.

Google Gemini ChromaDB

⚡ Intelligent RAG Engine

Retrieval workflows harness powerful LLMs to orchestrate answers grounded completely in local data. Endpoints isolate the prompt with strict bounds preventing hallucinations while synthesizing answers heavily referencing [SOURCE N] exact citation strings.
